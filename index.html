About

Led by Bharath Ramasubban

Mylai4 Advisory LLC was founded in 2024 to help organizations — from Fortune 500 enterprises to ambitious startups — harness the full potential of data and AI to drive real business outcomes.

Bharath brings over two decades of experience spanning enterprise consulting, supply chain technology, cloud platforms, and digital transformation. He has led large-scale transformation programs across retail, consumer goods, manufacturing, and supply chain — earning a reputation for bridging the gap between deep technology and commercial results.

The name Mylai4 is inspired by Mylapore, Chennai — a nod to roots and the belief that the best advisory comes from genuine understanding, not just frameworks. The approach is straightforward: understand the business first, apply the right technology, and drive measurable outcomes.
